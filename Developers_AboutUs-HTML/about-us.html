Company Background

The 4MnS is a competitive retail business that deals in the distribution and sales of consumer electronics, commercial and industrial products, home accessories, and appliances. The first 4MnS Appliances store was opened in 2020 in Kalusugan, Quezon City. It was a collaborative idea of Magramo, G., Magpantay, J.R., Marzan, R., Mendoza, A. and Sambo, G. hence the name 4MnS Appliances.

It started out as a means to help the sudden effect of the COVID-19 crisis to the slowing economic activity. As more and more people are embracing social distancing, the development of this e-commerce website has helped countless families on having access to the online shopping market more than ever before. This has become to be one of the reasons why our company is known for providing convenient and fast services to our customers with the use of emerging technologies.
